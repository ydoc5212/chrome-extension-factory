# Stopping Malicious Browser Extensions: Understand and Mitigate the Risk in Enterprise Environments

## Signal extracted

<!-- Fill in during curation. The one insight this post has that's hard to get elsewhere. -->

---

Browser extensions are routinely granted extensive permissions, **allowing access to users’ browsing information, passwords, cookies, and more**, exposing users to risks such as credential theft, account takeover, and data theft.

This risk is **further compounded in enterprise environments**, as exposure of corporate credentials can lead to data breaches that impact the entire organization, its employees, and customers. 

**Many security professionals are not sufficiently aware of this danger** and don’t have the appropriate skills and tools to evaluate their risk and take proper precautions to mitigate it.

Join this webinar by LayerX hosting browser extension security expert Matt Frisbie, to discuss the risks of browser extensions in corporate environments and **what you can do to minimize your risk while still using legitimate extensions.**

What You’ll Learn:

-   **Discover the Scope**: What are the main components of browser extensions, and what type of access they typically have to users’ endpoints.
-   **Understand the Risk**: Learn about types of data that can be stolen by malicious browser extensions and the potential impact on the organization. 
-   **Actionable Insights**: Gain practical, actionable steps to mitigate the risk of data leakage without compromising the productivity benefits of legitimate extensions.
-   **Real-World Solutions**: Hear from industry experts on steps you can take and tools you can use to address this challenge and stop malicious browser extensions from stealing your organization’s data.

This webinar is a must-attend for CISOs, security professionals, compliance officers, and anyone invested in securing their organizations.

Don't let malicious browser extensions compromise your business. **Register today**!
